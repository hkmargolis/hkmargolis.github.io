Facts about Mars

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun
  • Mars has a radius of 2,106 miles (2,290 kilometers), about half the size of Earth
  • Mars completes a rotation around the sun every 24.6 hours.
  • Mars has two small potato-shaped moons.
  • Mars is home to the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.

Reference: Nasa Website
