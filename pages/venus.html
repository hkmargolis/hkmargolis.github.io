Facts About Venus

  • Venus is the second planet from the sun.
  • Venus is the sixth, largest planet.
  • Venus is the hottest plant in our solar system.
  • A Venusian day is longer than its year. One day on Venus is 243 Earth days, while a year is only 225 Earth days.
  • Venus is often called Earth's twin because they are similar in size and structure.
